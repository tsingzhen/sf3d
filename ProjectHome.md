sf3d is a 3d engine based on SFML API.

## Features ##
  * Scene management
  * Multipass rendering
  * Mesh files (.obj, .md2)
  * Materials and textures
  * Static lights
  * Terrain generation with heightmaps
  * GLSL : Phong per-pixel lighting
